The place

As a promising online meeting/management/market platform, Counekt seeks to provide entrepreneurs, freelancers and likeminded with a flexible network, and a direct access to DAOs (decentralized autonomous organizations) and their exchange, thereby offering them the tools to bring their efforts to fruition.

Currently, we are testing the following leap-of-faith assumptions.

  • Value hypothesis: Users will engage with the networking map to enhance their professional connections. Moreover, they will participate in decentralized autonomous organizations and make use of the associated marketplace on the site.
  • Growth hypothesis: The platform will grow using the viral engine of growth and generate value through a transaction-based revenue model, assuming a significant amount of users trade on the marketplace.
